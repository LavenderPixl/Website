Dansk Vinimport - DVI


The program was created, to monitor certain information, in a warehouse.
It displays the temperature and humidity, aswell as how many wares are under minimum and need to be restocked, how many are over maximum, and how many have been sold, that day.
This information is updated every 5 minutes.
It also displays Date and Time, in Copenhagen, London and Singapore, which gets updated every second.
